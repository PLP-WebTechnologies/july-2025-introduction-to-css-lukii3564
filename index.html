Meet Your Instructor

Hello! My name is Luki, and I’ll be your guide in understanding the CSS Box Model. Think of me as your friendly teacher showing you how margins, borders, and padding shape every web layout.

Why the Box Model Matters

In these sessions, I, Teacher Luki, explain how every HTML element is wrapped in a box. By controlling padding, borders, and margins, you gain full mastery of spacing and alignment on a webpage.

Luki’s Teaching Style

I believe in a hands-on approach. Together, we’ll experiment with background colors, play with spacing, and visualize how each part of the Box Model works. By the end, you’ll style layouts with confidence!
